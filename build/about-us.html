About us

Overview

AMERI Holdings, Inc. is a fast-growing technology services company that specializes in SAP and Google cloud solutions.

Headquartered in Suwanee, GA, Ameri100 has offices in Atlanta, Dallas, Phoenix, Kansas City, Folsom, and Toronto, in addition to global delivery centers in India.

Using a bespoke engagement model, Ameri100 delivers transformational value to its clients across industry verticals.

Our Partners

We’ve established relationships with key technology partners who can complement our service offerings, drive higher margin solution sales, and help our customers become intelligent enterprises.

Corporate Social

Amazing things happen when our country’s servicemen and women apply the values they’ve developed in the military to a rewarding career path as a civilian. Ameri100 seeks to support veterans through their transition from the military into the civilian workforce by recruiting former military servicemen and women at every level of our organization. Once a hire has been made, Ameri100 works alongside Workforce Opportunity Services to continue to support a smooth transition.
